About Chancellor Chambers

A premier full-service law firm providing exceptional legal representation with integrity, strategic insight, and personalized attention.

Chukwuma Ezeala, B.L., LL.M, ACIArb

With over 15 years of experience in corporate law and dispute resolution, Mr. Ezeala has established himself as one of Nigeria's most respected legal practitioners. His expertise spans complex commercial transactions, arbitration, and regulatory compliance matters.

After earning his LL.M from the prestigious University of Lagos and being called to the Nigerian Bar, Mr. Ezeala honed his skills at leading international firms before founding Chancellor Chambers to provide world-class legal services with a distinctly Nigerian perspective.

Our firm combines meticulous attention to detail with creative problem-solving to deliver optimal outcomes for clients ranging from multinational corporations to high-net-worth individuals.

Chukwuma Ezeala, Esq.
