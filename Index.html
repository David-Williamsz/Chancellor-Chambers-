About The Senior Partner

Chukwuma Ezeala is the Senior Partner at Chancellor Chambers, with over 15 years of experience in corporate law, commercial litigation, and alternative dispute resolution. His expertise spans across multiple jurisdictions, providing strategic legal counsel to both local and international clients.

Called to the Nigerian Bar in 2007, Chukwuma holds a Bachelor of Laws (LL.B) from the University of Lagos, a Master of Laws (LL.M) in International Commercial Law from the University of London, and is an Associate of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (ACIArb).

Known for his meticulous approach and commitment to excellence, Chukwuma has successfully handled complex legal matters for multinational corporations, financial institutions, and high-net-worth individuals across Africa.
